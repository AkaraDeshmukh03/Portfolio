Shop-hub Cart

Project Information

  • Category: Product Design
  • Client: Troopod
  • Timeline: Feb 2023 - Present
  • Project URL: troopod.io

Crafted with precision, the Mini-Cart integration seamlessly complements the user journey, providing essential information at a glance. Each element, from product listing to offer display and bill summary, was meticulously designed through a process of thorough research and iterative refinement. The product cards, displaying vital information like image, name, variant selector, quantity toggle, and dynamic pricing, reflect our commitment to a user-centric approach. The offers panel empowers users with the ability to search and apply coupons effortlessly, ensuring a seamless checkout experience. The coupon cards, thoughtfully designed, provides users a clear view of available discounts. The bill summary section, curates a comprehensive view of the total cost, discounts, delivery charges, applied coupons, and the grand total. This integration not only enhances user convenience but also streamlines the path to purchase, resulting in an enriched shopping experience for our clients and their customers.
