Shop-hub CTA

Project Information

  • Category: Product Design
  • Client: Troopod
  • Timeline: Feb 2023 - Present
  • Project URL: troopod.io

Revolutionising the e-commerce experience, I took off on the project to enhance the Call-to-Action (CTA) section. Positioned strategically below the price tag, it offers a versatile widget with an array of options*, from Percentage Off to Warranty Information. Through meticulous research and extensive user testing, I refined the design iteratively, ensuring optimal functionality and user engagement. This innovative feature empowers our clients to showcase key information, elevating their customer experience.

  • *Options being
  • 1. Percentage Off
  • 2. Shipping Information
  • 3. Coupon Code Availability and Discount Details
  • 4. Delivery Information
  • 5. Card-Based Price Cash Backs
  • 6. Coupon-Based Cash Backs
  • 7. Limited Time Offers
  • 8. Warranty Information
  • 9. And many more
