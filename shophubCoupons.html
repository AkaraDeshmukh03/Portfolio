Shop-hub Coupons

Project Information

  • Category: Product Design
  • Client: Troopod
  • Timeline: Feb 2023 - Present
  • Project URL: troopod.io

    The Offers section stands as a testament to thoughtful design and strategic insight. Drawing inspiration from current market trends, I delved into a process of iteration and refinement to create a dynamic array of options. From subscriptions to percentage-based and price-based coupons, card-based cash backs, to bundled product offerings, each offer is meticulously tailored to suit diverse user preferences.
    The design of the offer cards is not just about aesthetics, but also about providing users with a visual cue to easily grasp the value proposition. These cards adapt seamlessly to different offer types, ensuring a clear and engaging visual representation.
    Within a compact 300px height, the horizontally scrollable section effortlessly integrates all vital information. Users can swiftly evaluate the applied offer, access an apply button, find the relevant coupon code or offer title, and gain a concise understanding of their savings. This seamless integration enhances the user experience, making the path to purchase not only lucrative but also visually intuitive.
